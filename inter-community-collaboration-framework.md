# Python Bangladesh: Inter-Community Collaboration & Growth Framework

This document outlines the shared vision, principles, and collaboration areas between **Python Bangladesh** and **like-minded technology communities**.  
It serves as a mutual understanding to foster long-term collaboration, sustainability, and collective growth within the Python and open-source ecosystem in Bangladesh.

This is **not a legal contract**, but a community-driven framework based on trust, transparency, and shared values.

---

## üéØ Purpose

The purpose of this framework is to:

- Connect independent tech communities under a shared national umbrella  
- Encourage collaboration rather than isolation  
- Enable shared learning, resources, and infrastructure  
- Strengthen the overall Python and open-source ecosystem in Bangladesh  

---

## üå± Shared Values

All collaborating communities are expected to align with the core values inspired by the **Python Software Foundation (PSF)**:

- Open Source and Open Knowledge  
- Open Access and inclusivity  
- Community-led, volunteer-driven growth  
- Respectful, harassment-free environments

## üß≠ Autonomy & Responsibilities

- Each community remains **independent and self-governed**
- Python Bangladesh does **not interfere** in internal leadership or decision-making
- Communities are responsible for:
  - Their content and activities  
  - Maintaining respectful conduct  
  - Keeping shared resources used responsibly  

---

## üõ° Code of Conduct Alignment

All collaborating communities must maintain a **Code of Conduct** aligned with the  
**Python Bangladesh Code of Conduct**:

https://github.com/pythonbangladesh/public-docs/blob/main/code-of-conduct.md

This ensures a safe, inclusive, and welcoming environment across all shared activities.

---

## üîÑ Review & Continuity

- Collaboration may be reviewed periodically to ensure alignment with shared values
- Either party may step back from collaboration if:
  - Community values diverge  
  - Shared resources are misused  
  - The community becomes inactive or commercial  

Such decisions should be handled transparently and respectfully.
